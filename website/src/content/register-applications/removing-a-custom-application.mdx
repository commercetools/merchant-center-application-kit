---
title: Remove a Custom Application
---

In case the Custom Application is of no use for your project anymore, you can remove it. To remove a Custom Application:

1. Open a Custom Application's detail page by clicking on the **Edit** button.

2. Click the **Delete** button in the upper right to remove the application.

  ![Delete button](/images/mc-apps-removal.png)

3. In the confirmation dialog, click **Confirm**.

The application is removed from the project.
